## Extract licenses information

This check extracts high-level licenses information from your org to support capacity planning and governance.

It complements the unused licenses check by giving a broader view of current license usage.

Sfdx-hardis command: [sf hardis:org:diagnose:licenses](https://sfdx-hardis.cloudity.com/hardis/org/diagnose/licenses/)

Key: **LICENSES**
